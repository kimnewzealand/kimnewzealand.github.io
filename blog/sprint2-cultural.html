Learning Reflections

from the Foundations course at the Dev Academy

23 April 2020
juggling /

What is your long term goal and or career pathway?

I would like to continue to work with startups and SMEs to solve data problems, build open source communities and one day possibly be a co-founder.

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at EDA?

I would like to develop my technical coding skills, as there are some gaps in my foundations as I am self taught in coding. For example the command line is so powerful but I haven't yet used it's full potential.
In terms of human skills, I would like to use the opportunity to work with different teams outside of my current data spheres and practice pair programming which I haven't done before.

A description of your own strengths and limitations

My strengths are curiosity and risk taking, I am always looking out for opportunities; balanced as I can bring calm and structure to chaotic situations; purposeful as I enjoy keeping busy, and can problem solve even in chaotic situations.
My limitations are that I can get impatient with myself and get bored if I'm not busy. I need to work more on spending time on self care.

Based on the above description, a commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme. This should include - an explanation of how you will manage yourself to work productively and safely with other learners, facilitators and industry/community representatives.

I plan to follow the sprints week to week. I will also provide feedback to as many of my cohort as possible, whether it's on challenges in Slack or on projects during the bootcamp.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way.

Yesterday I lost track of my time so I'm going to consciously work on the pomodoro forward and follow the timer.

I will work through an issue or bug within a pomodoro and seek help on Slack at the end of the timebox if needed.

A description of what you expect from the facilitation team

To be easily reachable for any questions or support during Foundation daylight hours.
To call out with any suggestions to change course or direction.

Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things.

I have school age children, so may need to do school drop offs or pickups.
