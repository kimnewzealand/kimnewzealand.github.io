About me

Background

I have a background in Actuarial Science and have worked in analyst roles and project delivery in Credit Risk and Finance.

I discovered data science as a magical field that combines my statistics and analytical skills; my project and multitasking experience; a curiosity of numbers and patterns; love of learning new tools and concepts whilst working with a variety of people and teams.

Current Work

I now work as an independent through Validly and am available for remote data science projects.

Recent client projects and professional interests include:

  • Data discovery and analysis in R
  • Interactive visualisations with R, D3 and JavaScript
  • Time series in R
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) in R and Python
  • Geospatial analysis in R and QGIS

R-Ladies Auckland Meetup

I founded the Auckland chapter of R-Ladies in November 2017. I was inspired by attending the R-Ladies Melbourne meetup in October 2017. Over and above supporting the R-Ladies mission, I would like to encourage those who have had career breaks or would like to change mid-career involved in the community.

Manifesto for Data Practices

I have signed the Manifesto for Data Practices. These values and principles, taken together, describe the most effective, ethical, and modern approach to data teamwork.
