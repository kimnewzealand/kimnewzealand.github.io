About me

Background

I have a background in Actuarial Science and have worked in project delivery in Credit Risk and Finance. I discovered data science as a magical field that combines my statistics and analytical skills; my project and multitasking experience; a curiosity of numbers and patterns; learning new tools whilst working with a variety of people and teams.

Current Role

I now work as an independent through Validly and am currently available for remote data science work.

Other Professional Activities

I regularly attend a range of meetups across Auckland and data science conferences. I started the Auckland chapter of R-Ladies Auckland in November 2017 after attending the R-Ladies Melbourne meetup in October 2017.

My current areas interest for professional development are:

interactive visualisations in R and JavaScript

time series in R

machine learning in R, Keras and H2O
